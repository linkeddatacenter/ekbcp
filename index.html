Welcome to EKBCP

EKBCP project provides an interactive control panel for LinkedData.Center web services and was initially developed by Turtle Team during wikimedia 2016 in LinkedData.Center Campus in Esino Lario (Italy).

EKBCP uses YASGUI and 3Dsparql libraries integrated with the EKB APIs developed by LinkedData.Center

Have a look to our EKBCP service at http://cpanel.linkeddata.center/. You can use the read only LinkedData.Center demo credentials (https://hub1.linkeddata.center/demo, demo, demo) or appy for a free tier subcription
